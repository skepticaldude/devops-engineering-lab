## Course Overview

This course equips students with the knowledge needed to build **highly reliable and efficient solutions on Google Cloud** using proven design patterns.

It serves as a continuation of the following courses:

- **Architecting with Google Compute Engine**
- **Architecting with Google Kubernetes Engine**

Because of this, the course assumes participants already have **hands-on experience with the technologies covered in those programs**.

---

## What You Will Learn

Through a combination of:

- Presentations
- Design activities
- Hands-on labs

participants will learn how to **define and balance both business and technical requirements** in order to design Google Cloud deployments that are:

- Highly reliable
- Highly available
- Secure
- Cost-effective

---

## Course Goal

By the end of this course, learners will be able to design **robust Google Cloud architectures** that meet real-world operational and business needs.
