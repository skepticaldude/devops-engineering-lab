# Why This Course Exists

Have you ever experienced situations like these?

- You're concerned about the **reliability of your services**.
- Customer engagement declines, but your teams cannot explain why.

- Conflicting priorities between **development and operations teams**

These situations often happen because **teams work in silos** and pursue different goals.

---

# Google's Approach: Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)

Google has spent years operating systems at **massive scale**.

Over time, Google standardized practices that balance:

- **Feature velocity**
- **System reliability**

These practices, combined with a supporting culture, are called:

**Site Reliability Engineering (SRE)**.

SRE helps organizations:

- Improve service reliability
- Balance development speed with operational stability
- Align development and operations teams
