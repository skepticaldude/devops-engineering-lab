# Assessing Your Organization for SRE Adoption

Throughout the previous modules, you have learned about **Google's SRE practical and cultural fundamentals** and ways to adopt them in your organization.

Before implementing many of these technical principles, it is important to consider **your organization’s maturity and readiness** for SRE adoption.

---

## Module Objectives

In this module, you will learn:

1. **Assessing organizational readiness**  
   - How prepared your organization is to adopt SRE principles, practices, and culture.

2. **Required SRE skills and training**  
   - Key skills needed for SRE roles  
   - Strategies to train and upskill your current workforce

3. **SRE team implementations**  
   - Examples of different SRE team structures  
   - How **Google Cloud Professional Services** can support your journey to SRE adoption
